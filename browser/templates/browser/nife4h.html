Group 4h [NiFe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [NiFe] Group 4: Respiratory H2-evolving [NiFe] hydrogenases
Subgroup [NiFe] Group 4h: Ferredoxin-coupled, Eha-type
Function Forms supercomplex that couples H2 oxidation to ferredoxin reduction by utilising the sodium-motive force. The reduced ferredoxin generated can be used anaplerotically to replenish the Wolfe cycle of methanogenesis. While the reverse direction is energetically favourable, it is unclear whether it occurs physiologically.
Activity H2-uptake
Oxygen Tolerance O2-sensitive
Localisation Membrane-bound

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Hydrothermal systems, other high temperature ecosystems
Taxonomic Distribution Sporadically distributed in anaerobic bacteria and archaea
