Group 2b [NiFe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [NiFe] Group 2: Alternative and sensory H2-uptake [NiFe] hydrogenases
Subgroup [NiFe] Group 2b: Histidine kinase-linked
Function H2-sensing in regulatory cascades. Enzyme transfers H2-liberated electrons to PAS domain. Associated redox change modulates histidine kinase activity and in turn the two-component system controlling expression of primary H2-uptake hydrogenases.
Activity H2-sensing
Oxygen Tolerance O2-tolerant
Localisation Cytosolic

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Various soil and aquatic samples
Taxonomic Distribution Exclusive to aerobic Proteobacteria
