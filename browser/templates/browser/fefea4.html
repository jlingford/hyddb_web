Group A4 [FeFe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [FeFe]-hydrogenases
Subgroup [FeFe] Group A4: Formate dehydrogenase-linked
Function Most complexes appear to couple oxidation of formate to fermentative evolution of H2. Several complexes are multifunctional bifurcating complexes that can use the electrons from H2 or formate to simultaneously reduce NADP and ferredoxin.
Activity H2-evolution or electron-bifurcation
Oxygen Tolerance O2-labile
Localisation Cytosolic

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Gastrointestinal tracts, anoxic soils and sediments
Taxonomic Distribution Present in some Firmicutes
