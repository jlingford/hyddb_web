Group 4i [NiFe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [NiFe] Group 4: Respiratory H2-evolving [NiFe] hydrogenases
Subgroup [NiFe] Group 4i: Ferredoxin-coupled, Ehb-type
Function Forms supercomplex that couples H2 oxidation to ferredoxin reduction by utilising the sodium-motive force. The reduced ferredoxin generated can be used anabolically to sustain biosynthetic reactions. While the reverse direction is energetically favourable, it is unclear whether it occurs physiologically.
Activity H2-uptake
Oxygen Tolerance O2-sensitive
Localisation Membrane-bound

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Methanogenic environments such as anoxic soils, marine sediments, and gastrointestinal tracts
Taxonomic Distribution Encoded in multiple methanogenic orders
