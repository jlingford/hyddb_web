Group 4d [NiFe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [NiFe] Group 4: Respiratory H2-evolving [NiFe] hydrogenases
Subgroup [NiFe] Group 4d: Ferredoxin-coupled, Mrp-linked
Function Forms respiratory supercomplex that couples oxidation of ferredoxinred to reduction of protons to H2. The energy released is used to translocate Na+ ions through Mrp-like antiporter subunits to generate a sodium-motive force.
Activity H2-evolution
Oxygen Tolerance O2-sensitive
Localisation Membrane-bound

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Hydrothermal vents, other high temperature ecosystems
Taxonomic Distribution Exclusive to Thermococci and Aciduliprofundi
