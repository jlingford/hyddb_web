Group 1b [NiFe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [NiFe] Group 1: Respiratory H2-uptake [NiFe] hydrogenases
Subgroup [NiFe] Group 1b: Prototypical
Function Hydrogenotrophic respiration using sulfate, fumarate, nitrate, metals, and azo compounds as terminal electron acceptors. Enzyme transfers H2-liberated electrons through cytochromes to terminal reductase.
Activity H2-uptake (unidirectional)
Oxygen Tolerance O2-sensitive
Localisation Membrane-bound or periplasmic

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Anoxic soils and sediments, marine waters, animal gastrointestinal tracts
Taxonomic Distribution Widespread in Deltaproteobacteria and Epsilonproteobacteria
