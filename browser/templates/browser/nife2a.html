Group 2a [NiFe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [NiFe] Group 2: Alternative and sensory H2-uptake [NiFe] hydrogenases
Subgroup [NiFe] Group 2a: Cyanobacteria-type
Function Hydrogenotrophic respiration using O2 as the terminal electron acceptor. Important for recycling of H2 produced by nitrogenase and fermentation. Route of electron transfer unresolved.
Activity H2-uptake (unidirectional, some high-affinity)
Oxygen Tolerance Some O2-tolerant
Localisation Membrane-associated?

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Diverse soil and aquatic environments
Taxonomic Distribution Widespread among Cyanobacteria and aerobic soil bacteria (e.g. Actinobacteria)
