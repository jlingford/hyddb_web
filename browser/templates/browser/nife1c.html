Group 1c [NiFe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [NiFe] Group 1: Respiratory H2-uptake [NiFe] hydrogenases
Subgroup [NiFe] Group 1c: Hyb-tolerant
Function Hydrogenotrophic respiration using fumarate, sulfate, or metals as terminal electron acceptors. Route of electron transfer unresolved. Can also fermentatively evolved H2 under hypoxia.
Activity H2-uptake (unidirectional)
Oxygen Tolerance O2-sensitive
Localisation Membrane-bound

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Animal gastrointestinal tracts, anoxic soils and sediments
Taxonomic Distribution Widespread in Enterobacteria and various sulfate-reducing bacteria
