Group A3 [FeFe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [FeFe]-hydrogenases
Subgroup [FeFe] Group A3: Bifurcating
Function Bifurcates electrons from H2 to NAD and ferredoxin. Reverse reaction can also occur resulting in reoxidation of redox carriers concomitant with fermentative H2 evolution.
Activity Electron-bifurcation
Oxygen Tolerance O2-labile
Localisation Cytosolic

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Gastrointestinal tracts, anoxic soils and sediments
Taxonomic Distribution Widespread in anaerobic bacteria, including Firmicutes, Bacteroidetes, Deltaproteobacteria, and Spirochaetes
