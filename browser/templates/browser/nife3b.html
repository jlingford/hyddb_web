Group 3b [NiFe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [NiFe] Group 3: Cofactor-coupled bidirectional [NiFe] hydrogenases
Subgroup [NiFe] Group 3b: NADP-coupled
Function Directly couples oxidation of NADPH to fermentative evolution of H2. Reverse reaction may also occur. Some complexes controversially proposed to harbour sulfhydrogenase activity.
Activity Bidirectional
Oxygen Tolerance O2-tolerant
Localisation Cytosolic

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Diverse soil and aquatic environments
Taxonomic Distribution Encoded in multiple diverse bacterial and archaeal phyla

Important Notes

Despite the ubiquitous nature of this hydrogenase, the enzyme has only been purified from Pyrococcus furiosus. It remains unclear whether bacterial enzymes are also NADP+-specific.
