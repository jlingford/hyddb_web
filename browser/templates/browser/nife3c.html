Group 3c [NiFe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [NiFe] Group 3: Cofactor-coupled bidirectional [NiFe] hydrogenases
Subgroup [NiFe] Group 3c: Heterodisulfide reductase-linked
Function Bifurcates electrons from H2 to heterodisulfide and ferredoxin. This is the final step in the Wolfe cycle of methanogenesis.
Activity Electron-bifurcation
Oxygen Tolerance O2-tolerant?
Localisation Cytosolic

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Methanogenic environments such as anoxic soils, marine sediments, and gastrointestinal tracts
Taxonomic Distribution Encoded in most methanogens and sulfate-reducing bacteria
