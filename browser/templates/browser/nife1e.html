Group 1e [NiFe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [NiFe] Group 1: Respiratory H2-uptake [NiFe] hydrogenases
Subgroup [NiFe] Group 1e: Isp-type
Function Hydrogenotrophic respiration using sulfur as terminal electron acceptor. Route of electron transfer unresolved. Complex can also evolve H2 using sulfur-derived electrons possibly through reversed electron flow or electron-bifurcation.
Activity Bidirectional (possibly electron-bifurcating)
Oxygen Tolerance O2-sensitive (some tolerance in microoxic conditions)
Localisation Membrane-bound

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Soil and aquatic systems with high sulfur content
Taxonomic Distribution Present in phototrophic and chemotrophic bacteria capable of respiratory sulfur oxidation and reduction
