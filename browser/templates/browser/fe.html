[Fe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [Fe]-hydrogenases
Subgroup N/A
Function Directly couples oxidation of H2 to reduction of 5,10-methenyltetrahydromethanopterin during hydrogenotrophic methanogenesis. Reverse reaction proposed to occur during formatotrophic methanogenesis.
Activity Bidirectional
Oxygen Tolerance O2-tolerant
Localisation Cytosolic

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Methanogenic environments such as anoxic soils, marine sediments, and gastrointestinal tracts
Taxonomic Distribution Encoded in multiple methanogenic orders
