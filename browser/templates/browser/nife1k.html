Group 1k [NiFe]-hydrogenases

Properties

Group [NiFe] Group 1: Respiratory H2-uptake [NiFe] hydrogenases
Subgroup [NiFe] Group 1k: Methanophenazine-reducing
Function Hydrogenotrophic respiration using heterodisulfide as a terminal electron acceptor. Enzyme transfers H2-liberated electrons through cyt b and methanophenazine to heterodisulfide reductase.
Activity H2-uptake (unidirectional)
Oxygen Tolerance O2-sensitive
Localisation Membrane-bound

Distribution

Ecosystem Distribution Methanogenic environments such as anoxic soils, marine sediments, and gastrointestinal tracts
Taxonomic Distribution Exclusive to methanogens of the order Methanosarcinales
